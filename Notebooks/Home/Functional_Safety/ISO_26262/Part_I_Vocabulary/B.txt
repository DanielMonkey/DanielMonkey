baseline
version of a set of one or more work products, items or elements that is under configuration management and used as a basis for further development through the change management process.

branch coverage
percentage of branches of the control flow that have been executed
NOTE 1 100% branch coverage implies 100% statement coverage
NOTE 2 An if-statement always has two branches - condition true and condition false - independent of the existence of an else-clause.
