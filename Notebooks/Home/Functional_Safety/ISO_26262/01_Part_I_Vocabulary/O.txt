Created Friday 22 March 2019

operating mode
perceivable functional state of an item or element
EXAMPLE System off; system active; system passive; degraded operation; emergency operation

operating time
cumulative time that an item or element is functioning

operational situation
scenario that can occur during a vehicle's life
EXAMPLE Driving; parking; maintenance;

other technology
technology different from E/E technologies within the scope of ISO 26262
EXAMPLE Mechanical technology; hydraulic technology
NOTE Other technologies can either be considered in the specification of the functional safety concept, during the allocation of safety requirements, or as an external measure
