technical safety concept
specification of the technical safety requirements and their allocation to system elements for implementation by the system design

technical safety requirement
requirement derived for implementation of associated functional safety requirements
NOTE The derived requirement includes requirements for mitigation.

testing
process of planning, preparing, and operating or exercising an item or an element to verify that it satisfies requirements, to detect anomalies, and to create confidence in its behavior

transient fault
fault that occurs once and subsequently disappears
NOTE Transient faults can appear due to electromagnetic interference, which can lead to bit-flips. Soft errors such as Single Event Upset (SEU) and Single Event Transient (SET) are transient faults.
