====== 05 Square roots ======
Created Tuesday 25 June 2019

Loops are often used in programs that compute numerical results by starting with an approximate answer and iteratively improving it.

In general we don't know ahead of time how many steps it takes to get the right answer, but we know when we get there because the estimate stops changing.
